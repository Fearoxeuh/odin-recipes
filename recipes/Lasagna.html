Lasagna

Lasagna is both a pasta in the shape of rectangular sheets, and an Italian cuisine recipe based on alternating layers of lasagna pasta, parmesan, mozzarella, or ricotta, and bolognese sauce or béchamel sauce, gratinated in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Grated Cheese
  • Grated Nutmeg
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato Puree
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Lasagna Dough
  • Clove Garlic
  • Celery stalk
  • Ground Beef
  • Water
  • Basil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Brown chopped garlic cloves and sliced ​​onions in a little olive oil.
  2. Add the carrot and the chopped celery stalk then the meat and brown everything.
  3. After a few minutes, add the red wine. Leave to cook until evaporated.
  4. Add the tomato puree, water and herbs. Add salt and pepper, then simmer over low heat for 45 minutes.
  5. Prepare the béchamel: melt 100 g of butter.
  6. Off the heat, add the flour all at once.
  7. Return to the heat and stir with a whisk until you obtain a very smooth mixture.
  8. Add the milk little by little.
  9. Stir constantly until the mixture thickens.
  10. Then, flavor with nutmeg, salt and pepper. Cook for about 5 minutes, over very low heat, stirring.
  11. Preheat the oven to 200°C (thermostat 6-7). Oil the lasagna dish. Place a thin layer of béchamel then lasagna sheets, bolognese, béchamel and parmesan. Repeat the operation 3 times in a row.
  12. On the last layer of lasagna, put only béchamel and cover with grated cheese. Sprinkle a few knobs of butter.
  13. Bake for approximately 25 minutes.
  14. Taste!
